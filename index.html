What is Kanban?

by Mykola Nazimkov

Kanban - is a lean method to manage and improve work across human systems

In software development, the aim is to provide a visual process management system which aids decision-making about what, when, and how much to produce.

Three general practices of Kanban:

  1. It visualizes the work of the development team (the features and user stories).
  2. It captures WIP limits for development steps: the circled values below the column headings that limit the number of work items under that step
  3. It documents policies, also known as done rules, inside blue rectangles under some of the development steps.
